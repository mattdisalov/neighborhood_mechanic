About

We are a service providing a map of Auto Repair, Tire and Auto Body Shops in Broward County, FL.

Each category is represented by a different icon. If you click on the icon of a specific shop, a pop-up window will show you details about the shop, such as Name, Category, Reviews, Address, Website, Phone Number and Hours.

If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to contact us!
